Welcome to Minesweeper!

Instructions

How to play Minesweeper:
1. There are 15 'mines' hidden throughout this 10x10 board
2. Click to reveal a square
3. Right click to flag/mark a square
4. Click a square to start the timer
5. The numbers in the revealed squares represent
the number of mines in the surrounding 8 squares
6. flag all of the mines in the fastest time possible
7. If you reveal a mine, YOU LOSE!

Game Features

1. Scoreboard will update as you win games
2. You can change the number of mines you would
like in the submission on the right side
(between 1-99). To correctly change the number of mines,
pick a number, press submit, then restart the game
